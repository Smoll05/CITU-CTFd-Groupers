Cookie Games!

Here are some fun games to play that involve cookies:

  • Cookie counting game: Count the number of cookies in a jar and see who gets the closest guess.
  • Cookie decorating: Unleash your creativity and decorate your own cookies with frosting and sprinkles.
  • Cookie scavenger hunt: Hide cookies around the house and let everyone join in the search.
